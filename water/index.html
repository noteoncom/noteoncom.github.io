Water Clock

Water Clock is a simple log app designed to track, analyze daily water intake and remind to keep good habit and health.

TRACK

  • Customizing beverages other than water can help you record your intake of water, caffeine, alcohol, and energy.
  • Also, using these drinks to define cups with different capacities can help you quickly create records.
  • If necessary, you can also manually manage the logs, including adding, deleting, and updating the drink, volume and notes in the logsã€‚

ANALYTICS

  • Daily analytics can help you understand the total amount of water intake, time period, and proportion of different drinks in a day.
  • Water Clock also provides the analytics on a weekly, monthly, and annual time span.
  • It is convenient to understand the daily average, maximum, and minimum water consumption over a period of time, as well as the distribution of various beverages.

REMINDER

According to your personal profile and daily habits, the app will automatically set daily water reminders. Of course, you can also customize the water drinking reminder, time, or prompt.
