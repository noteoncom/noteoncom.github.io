Weekly Plan

Simple and efficient weekly plan.

Easy to use:

  • Easy to create by tapping the blank.
  • Instant edit mode when tapping on the task.
  • Instantly manage the project, schedule.

Manage the task:

  • Add remarks to a task.
  • Schedule the task, edit start, end or due date. And reminder.
  • Group the tasks by project.
  • It's easy to search tasks through Spotlight Search.
  • And the dark mode is very cool. It's good to focus on the content (tasks).
