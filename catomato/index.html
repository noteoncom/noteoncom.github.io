Catomato

Another different tomato timer.

Features for tomato timer:

  • Put the iPhone face down or tap to Start.
  • Keep focus and break round by round automatically.
  • Free to setup the time for focus, break and number of rounds.

As a time logger:

  • Create a activity for each focus time automatically.
  • Easy to organize the activities, setup goals through tap or long press.
  • Setup and archive the goal.
